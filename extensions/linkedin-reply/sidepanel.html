0nLinkedIn

Click the extension icon to open the reply generator.

Or click the ⚡ 0n Reply button that appears on LinkedIn posts.

Quick Start

1. Go to Settings tab → paste your free Groq API key

2. Browse LinkedIn → click ⚡ 0n Reply on any post

3. Or use Find Posts tab to discover trending content

Tone Options

🧠 Thoughtful · 💪 Supportive · 🔥 Contrarian · 🎓 Expert · 😄 Funny

Pro Tip

Add your profile context in Settings so replies reference your expertise naturally.
